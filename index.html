The box is a message

DISTA is a company built around the idea of exchanging boxes between people, cities and cultures. For us, a box is not just packaging or a set of objects — it is a physical message from one place to another. We believe that a city or a country can be shared through small details: snacks, stickers, notes, printed materials, local symbols, strange souvenirs, handmade pieces and ordinary things that suddenly become meaningful in another context.

DISTA box is a personal version of a place. It does not try to explain a whole culture perfectly; instead, it collects fragments that show how a place can feel through someone’s choice, memory and everyday life.

Buy / exchange

You can buy a curated box from Russia, Japan or Abu Dhabi, or join a personal exchange with another participant. Commercial boxes are designed by our team and built from local objects, visual codes and cultural fragments, but every box stays slightly different because the objects come from real local sources.

Personal exchange is already available in selected countries. You cannot choose the exact sender or country — the system matches participants automatically. Check below to see whether your country is already part of the exchange network.

Designed to travel again

We treat the box as part of the exchange system, not as something disposable. After receiving a DISTA box, you can return the packaging to us: we clean it, reuse it or recycle the material for future drops. This way the box does not end its life after one delivery. It keeps moving, changing hands and becoming part of new exchanges.

For DISTA, sustainability is not just a label — it is a practical cycle where packaging can return, transform and travel again.
